Culture, Freedom, and Hospitality

Tigray and Ethiopia are proud of their culture and traditions. Ethiopia is famous as one of the only African countries that was never colonized. The people are known for their
kindness, coffee ceremonies, and warm welcome to guests. This spirit of independence and hospitality makes Aksum and Ethiopia a true symbol of African pride.
